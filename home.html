ABOUT US

Just as some people inherently radiate heat, so do some objects. No lipstick; with what they carry inside. At the western edge of Dražgoše stands Miklavževa house. It is simple and modest on the outside, but when you enter it, you are embraced by a warmth reminiscent of a mother's lap. Warmth, home smell, silence. You stop with her in an instant and you wish that this blissful feeling would never go away.

ACTIVITIES

Are you curious and like to explore new places? So that you won't be bored on your vacation in nature, we will introduce you to some activities that you should not miss with us. Let's start with Dražgoška gora, which you can climb in less than 30 minutes or you can ride your bike to Lake Bohinj, where you might meet some parachutist who took off right above the monument of the Battle of Dražgoška. Because it's winter up to 1m of snow falls, visitors like to go skiing on Rudna and Soriška planina. In addition to ski slopes, there are also cross-country trails on Jelovica and a toboggan run under Bičkovo skala.

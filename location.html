LOCATION

On the sunny side of the Alps!

THE DIVINE VIEW

The apartment is located in the small village of Dražgoše, in a beautiful environment of hills, forests and meadows. The village is an excellent starting point for hiking, cycling and to admire skydivers. Fresh mountain air and wind caught in the treetops, but nobody do not leave indifferent.

Our apartment offers 4 rooms, a shared kitchen and a space for socializing. In front of the apartment there is a beautiful view of the surrounding hills and it is nicely decorated surroundings with meadows, a garden and a field. We wish you a holiday spent as relaxed as possible, so there are no electronic devices in the apartment devices.

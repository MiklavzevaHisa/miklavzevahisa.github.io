COTTAGE

On the sunny side of the Alps!

JUST LIKE THE OLD TIMES

The apartment is located in the small village of Dražgoše, in a wonderful environment of hills, forests and meadows. The village is an excellent starting point for hiking and cycling and to admire paratroopers. Fresh mountain air and wind caught in the treetops and leaves no one indifferent, especially the paratroopers.

Our apartment offers 4 rooms, a shared kitchen and a space for socializing. In front of the apartment there is a beautiful view of the surrounding hills and beautifully landscaped surroundings with meadows, garden and field. We want you to spend your vacation to the fullest that relaxed therefore there are no electronic devices in the apartment.
